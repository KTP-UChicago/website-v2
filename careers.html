For Employers
Partnering with Kappa Theta Pi is an efficient way to engage with top talent at UChicago. We have students interested in pursuing internships and new grad full time positions in Software Engineering, Product Management, Quantitative Finance, Data Science, etc. If you are interested in this partnership or general engagement with our community via recruitment events, please do contact us. We respond promptly within 24 hours.
