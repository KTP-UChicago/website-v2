Frequently Asked Questions

Any UChicago student, who is not a member of any other technical fraternity on campus, is allowed to Rush KTP as long as they have at least 3 quarters remaining after the pledging process. In addition, we have a minimum GPA requirement of 2.00.

In the middle of the COVID-19 pandemic, a group of like-minded students got together to create a community of students interested in technology. As a result, our constitution was ratified on January 8th 2021.

Absolutely not! We welcome students from all majors. In fact, many of our active members have majors ranging from Economics to Molecular Engineering to Computer Science. We only require that you share a passion for technology whatever that means for you.

We have a vibrant community of students who are extremely passionate about technology. Many of our members have experience working for large tech giants such as Facebook, Microsoft and Apple. As a member of KTP, you will benefit from resume workshops, technical interview preparation and skill-building through committees, such as the Machine Learning committee, Committee on Hackathons, etc. You will also get a chance to engage with our upcoming community engagement projects.

There is no one-size-fits-all criterion we look for in our rushees. We embrace and are proud to have students across disciplines, genders, race, and other self-identifying characteristics. Having a diverse and inclusive community is at the core of our mission.
